  • What is this?

    This is an application that has two different uses:

    Instant Feed - Use tweets that contain the words you introduce to process a counting of all words and gives you the statistics

    Research Feed - Use tweets that contain the words you introduce but this time after your classification of some tweets the application will be able to do her own classification, giving you the counting of words separated by sentiment( good , bad )

  • How can I use this?

    To start is better to use Instant Feed that way you will be able to know if the words you want to use for tweets search are being used in twitter.

    Also, you are able to know what are the most used words in the tweets with that help you can use better words to get tweets for the research

    After you know the words you want to use in research you start doing a Research Feed you will have to introduce the words and how many tweets you want to get to evaluate.

    Done the evaluation the application will ask you words again and how many tweets you want the application to classify

    Done that you can access to the words counting and how many tweets the application determine as good or bad.

  • I cant start a Feed

    You only can start a feed one time after that you need to wait until he ends or he timeout

    To end he needs to reach the number of tweets you introduce

    To timeout he needs to be 30 seconds with no tweet arriving

  • I cant see graphs anymore

    If you do a new search Instant or Research you will clear all the tweets you had analyzed to prevent that instead of starting a new search if the subject is the same change the word classification or re-set the word probability and you will be able to re-use the data you had.

  • Instant Feed

  • What is Instant Feed?

    Instant Feed gives you the opportunity of knowing what are the most common words in twitter about some subject.

    Ex:

    Given the word's 'trump, usaelections'

    Every tweet that has the word trump or usaelections will be collected and divided into one word, two words and three words .

    All words will be counted and in that way you will be able to know which ones are the most common words used on tweets with word trump or usaelections

  • How do I start?

    To start click on the tab Instant Feed

    Write the word or word's in order to determine what tweets should be fetched from twitter also write how many tweets you want to use for analyze.

    After that you will be able to see the counting of words from each tweet that has the word or word's that you submit

  • I can't start a new feed

    You are only able to do a search at time, if you did a big search you have to wait until the end.

    If you did a small search but still you can't start a new feed you have to wait until the end or if the words you use are not in trend right now you have to wait 30 seconds . That's the time the application will wait for tweets with the words if non appear the feed stops automatically.

  • I introduce the words but no statistics

    If no tweet has reached the application with the words that you introduce no statistics to show.

    That means you used words that are not being used in twitter right now

    Try to see what are the trending words on twitter first.

  • Research Feed

  • How do I start?

    To start a new Research Feed press the tab Start Research.

    After introducing the word or words that you want to use in your tweet search and the number of tweets you want to classify press Get Learning Tweets

    When the search for tweets is done you will see the number of tweets you asked classify them as good or bad in your opinion.

    Press evaluation is done and the application will send you to the word probability page press Refresh.

    After pressing refresh all words will appear with the probability if you think some word should be more probably or less change it

    Press insert probability and the application will start to feed and classify the tweets containing the words you used to search

    After all that you will be able to see how many tweets the application classify as good or bad and also what are the most used words in the tweets

  • How do I know application guesses?

    If you click on All tweets/Change Evaluation you will be able to see all the tweets classified by the application. You can also change the classification in order to do a recount of words to give you the opportunity of change the probability table. If you do that new words will be added to the list and their probability and sentiment are based on your and application classification

  • How do add words to probability table

    After doing the research you can access the tweets classified by the application and change the sentiment application guessed. After insert in the application the tweets again you can see more words on the probability table you can also change the probability
